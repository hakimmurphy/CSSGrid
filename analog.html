The new world is digital so analog will become a thing of the past. So we think or it is apparant. Saving data to DNA strands because there are limited resources. From a music perspective no one records straight analog anymore. It is however a sought out sound that is easy yet expensive to implement. Fluffy, light, warm are objectives associated with this concept.
